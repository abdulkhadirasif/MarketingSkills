🤝Engagement & Community Building Strategy

Posting is only half the battle. Zensar should dedicate 30-40% of time to engagement and community building:

Daily Engagement Routine (45 minutes)

1

Respond to All Comments (10 min)

Reply to every comment on Zensar posts from the last 24 hours. Responses should add value, ask follow-up questions, or provide additional insights—not just "Thank you!"

2

Engage with Competitor & Thought Leader Content (15 min)

Identify 20-30 key accounts in the enterprise tech space (competitors, analysts, industry leaders, client companies). Comment meaningfully on 3-5 of their posts daily.

3

Share & Amplify Relevant Content (10 min)

Find 1-2 industry articles, research, or news that aligns with Zensar's expertise. Share with added commentary from Zensar's POV.

4

Relationship Building & DMs (10 min)

Send 2-3 personalized messages to new connections, thought leaders, or potential clients with genuine interest (not sales pitch).

Quality Comment Framework

Every Zensar comment should follow this pattern:

Pattern: [Insight] + [Connection] + [Question]

✓ "This resonates with what we're seeing in enterprise AI implementations. One thing we've learned is [specific insight]. How are you thinking about [challenge area]?"

✗ "Great post!" or "Totally agree!" (adds no value)

Community Building Initiatives

  • Host Monthly LinkedIn Live: Quarterly 15-20 minute live Q&A on trending topic (AI governance, transformation roadmap, industry trends)
  • Create a Series: "Transformation Tuesday" or "Friday Framework"—recurring posts that build audience anticipation
  • Leverage Polls: Weekly polls asking your audience about challenges, priorities, predictions. Use results to inform future content
  • Highlight Customer Voices: Repost and celebrate when customers/clients mention Zensar. Build social proof and relationships
  • Respond to Mentions: Set up alerts for Zensar mentions. Respond quickly and thoughtfully to join conversations

Distribution & Engagement Setup

  • Create list of 30 key accounts to engage with daily
  • Set up LinkedIn alerts for "Zensar" mentions

Analytics & Measurement

  • Capture baseline metrics (current followers, engagement rate, impressions, clicks)
  • Set up weekly analytics review process (who reviews? when?)
  • Create performance dashboard (Google Data Studio or simple Sheet)
  • Define success metrics for this month vs. 90 days
  • Plan monthly stakeholder report (cadence and format)
